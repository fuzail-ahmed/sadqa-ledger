The software helps catch some mistakes (like preventing a negative or zero amount) but cannot verify that what was typed matches what actually happened.

If you notice something that looks wrong — an amount that doesn't match what you paid, a payment that seems missing — please tell {{CONTACT_PERSON_NAME}} so it can be checked and corrected. That's normal and expected, not an accusation against anyone.
